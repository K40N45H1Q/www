A gentle and effective exterior cleaning system

What is DOFF Cleaning?

DOFF Cleaning uses superheated water and steam to remove dirt, moss, algae, mould, paint, graffiti and other stubborn deposits without damaging the surface.

Low pressure, high temperature and minimal water

Safe for Delicate Surfaces

Unlike conventional pressure washing, DOFF works at controlled low pressure. It is suitable for brick, natural stone, concrete, timber, terracotta, tiles and historic building façades.

Suitable for stubborn organic and surface deposits

Effective Contamination Removal

The system helps remove moss, algae, mould, dirt, graffiti and selected coatings while preserving the underlying material.

A controlled and efficient cleaning method

Fast Drying and Minimal Water Use

Because DOFF uses a low volume of superheated water, treated surfaces avoid excessive saturation and can dry much faster than with conventional pressure washing.
